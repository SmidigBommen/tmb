Weather Tips

  • Check forecasts daily: Meteo France, MeteoSwiss, ARPA Valle d'Aosta
  • Start hiking by 7-8 AM to reach passes before afternoon storms
  • Temperature drops ~1°C per 150m of altitude gained
  • Valley at 25°C can mean 15°C at the pass
  • Lightning: descend from ridges, avoid metal, crouch low
  • Carry warm layers even on sunny days ‚Äî conditions change fast

Mobile Coverage

  • Generally good in valleys and near towns
  • Poor/none: Courmayeur to Les Chapieux, Les Chapieux to Les Contamines
  • Download offline maps before departure (Maps.me, Gaia GPS)
  • Some refuges have WiFi ‚Äî don't count on it
  • Carry a portable battery charger (10,000+ mAh)
